# DemoCorp Overview

DemoCorp is a mid-size enterprise software company providing SaaS solutions to the healthcare and financial services industries. Founded in 2015, the company has grown to 200 employees across three offices (New York, London, Tokyo).

## Products

1. **BillingCore** — Enterprise billing platform (flagship product, 70% of revenue)
2. **DataSync** — Data integration middleware (20% of revenue)
3. **AnalyticsHub** — Business intelligence dashboard (10% of revenue)

## Strategic Initiatives

### Billing System Migration

DemoCorp is evaluating a migration from the legacy BillingCore (on-premise) to BillingCore Cloud (SaaS). The migration is expected to:

- Reduce invoice error rate from 2% to 0.5%
- Increase invoice capacity from 10,000 to 50,000 per month
- Require 6 months of development with 4 engineering teams
- Cost approximately $500,000 in migration expenses
- Generate $200,000 annual cost savings post-migration
